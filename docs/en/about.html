About

A personal approach with clear boundaries and real presence.

People do not only choose coaching content, but also the person guiding them. This page should therefore carry a strong story: why this work matters, which professionals, self-employed people, and people starting over you do it for, and how you guide without drama or hollow marketing.

This page is meant to build trust through a personal story, education, a short video introduction, a clear niche, and visible positioning.
Personal story

Why this work does not start with methods, but with attention and clarity.

This work starts from the belief that people move forward best when clear direction and real attention come together. Coaching and training are therefore not built around pressure, but around rhythm, honesty, and practical next steps that fit real life.

The approach is warm, but not vague. There is room for reflection, while action, boundaries, and accountability remain visible throughout the journey.

Positioning

What I do and what I deliberately do not do.

Yes 1-on-1 guidance, clear agreements, reflection linked to action.
No No everything-for-everyone offer, no floating vagueness, no mass coaching.
  • For ambitious professionals and self-employed people carrying a lot of responsibility
  • For people starting over who want to rebuild rhythm and confidence
  • For people ready for real follow-up, not just inspiration
  • Not intended as medical or clinical treatment
Video introduction

An authentic video makes this page stronger immediately.

A short personal video can strengthen this page immediately. It helps people understand who you work with, where clients usually get stuck, and how your sessions feel before they book an intake.

  • 60 to 90 seconds is enough
  • Filmed in natural light is perfectly fine
  • Focus on clarity, not polished production
