Stress & Burnout

Recovery asks for space and structure, not more pressure.

This guidance is for people who feel their system has been under tension for too long and who want to rebuild calm, overview, and energy.

We slow down where needed, but stay clear about what recovery asks and what that means in your week.
Your own visual for stress recovery
Recognizable signs

You feel you keep going, even when your body has been saying no for a long time.

  • You are tired, even after moments of rest
  • You worry a lot and feel little space in your head
  • You still say yes too often while your system is depleted
Approach

Recovery without vagueness or performance pressure.

  • Rebuild boundaries and planning
  • Daily routines that genuinely support recovery
  • Honest follow-up around energy, work, and capacity
