Insights

Content that shows your expertise and makes your niche findable.

The best coaching sites do not only live from design. They grow through content: blog articles, mini trainings, podcasts, FAQs, and case studies that show exactly who the site is meant for.

Use this page as the basis for a blog, podcast, or knowledge hub around your most valuable themes.

Blog & podcast

Do not write about 'coaching', but about the exact problems you solve.

Specific niches perform better in Google and feel more professional to visitors who recognize themselves.

SEO idee 01

What do you do when you function everywhere, but feel completely drained inside?

Ideal article for stress recovery and high-functioning overload. This is far sharper than simply saying 'stress coach'.

SEO idee 02

How do you learn to exercise again without performance pressure or guilt?

A strong angle for personal training for people restarting, beginners, and people with negative gym experiences.

SEO idee 03

Setting boundaries at work without becoming blunt or hard

Perfect for assertiveness, managers, employees, and women who often hold back in conversations.

SEO idee 04

Why you do have discipline, but still feel no calm or direction

A strong life coaching theme that clearly separates superficial motivation from real clarity.
